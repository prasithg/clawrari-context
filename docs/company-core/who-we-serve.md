# Who We Serve

## Primary: Technical Founders & CTOs

**Profile:** Running a startup or leading engineering. Already using OpenClaw (or want to). Treats their AI assistant as infrastructure, not a toy. Wants to level up from "basic chatbot" to "reliable operating system."

**Job to be done:** "I want a proven system for running my AI assistant so I can focus on building my company, not configuring my tools."

**What they get from Clawrari:**
- Work persona with enterprise integrations (Google, Slack, GitHub, coding agents)
- 8090-style structured development process baked in
- Morning briefings, responsive scans, task management out of the box
- Self-improvement loops that make the setup better without manual effort

## Secondary: AI Engineers & Power Users

**Profile:** Deep in the AI/agent ecosystem. Follows the space closely. Wants to study how a production-quality agent setup actually works — not just read about it.

**Job to be done:** "Show me how you actually run your assistant. Not the theory — the real files, the real workflows, the real mistakes."

**What they get from Clawrari:**
- A reference implementation to study and adapt
- Public decision logs and FRDs showing the "why" behind every choice
- Self-improvement patterns (regressions, predictions, autoresearch) they can adopt
- Content that teaches mental models, not just configurations

## Tertiary: Content Creators in the AI Space

**Profile:** Building their personal brand around AI engineering, agents, or developer productivity. Wants to "build in public" but doesn't have a system for it.

**Job to be done:** "I want my daily AI assistant usage to automatically generate content opportunities."

**What they get from Clawrari:**
- Personal persona with content engine, social engagement, brand building
- The "every feature shipped = a post" philosophy
- A public framework they can fork and adapt for their own brand
- Proof that the system works (Prasith's own content as case study)

## Who We Don't Serve

- **Non-technical users** — Clawrari requires comfort with CLIs, config files, and terminal workflows
- **Casual ChatGPT/Claude users** — this is for people who run a local AI assistant, not cloud chat interfaces
- **Enterprise IT buyers** — this isn't a product you purchase. It's an open source project you adopt.
- **People who want "set it and forget it"** — Clawrari rewards engagement and iteration
